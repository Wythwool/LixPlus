Server Rules

LixPlus Survival

Mode: Survival + MMO mechanics + World Bosses

TL;DR: Don’t be a jerk, don’t break the server, don’t exploit the system.

1. General

  1. By joining LixPlus you automatically accept these rules and any future revisions.
  2. “Didn’t know” is not a valid excuse.
  3. Staff may tweak rules or punishments at their discretion for edge-cases.

2. Survival Gameplay

  1. Safe vs. Wild: /spawn, markets and towns are PvP-free; everywhere else is open PvP.
  2. Unclaimed bases can be griefed; breaking into a claimed region = ban.
  3. Dupes, lag machines, or anything that drags TPS below 18 ⇒ warn ➜ ban.
  4. Mob cap: 20 entities per chunk, max 5 spawners in a 16-block radius.
  5. AFK farms: mechanical clock loops allowed; autoclickers, taped keys, scripts not allowed.

3. MMO Content & World Bosses

  1. Bosses spawn in a ±5 min window and must be killed within 20 мин.
  2. Loot logic
    • ≥ 30 % dmg → guaranteed personal drop
    • < 30 % dmg → rolls on public loot table
  3. Kill-steal (tagging boss only at the end) → 24 h ban.
  4. Dragging bosses into spawn/loading chunks or using collision glitches = ban.
  5. AFK XP grinding on dungeon mobs = 3-day ban.

4. Chat & Voice

  1. Swearing is fine in moderation. Hate speech, IRL politics or personal harassment = mute/ban.
  2. Spam (≥ 3 identical lines) → 30 min mute.
  3. Advertising other servers, malware links, NFT/crypto shills → permaban.

5. Clients, Mods, Software

AllowedGray-area (ask first)Instant ban

6. Economy & Trading

  1. Currency: LixCoin (LC).
  2. Real-money trades or “donor shop outside the store” → permaban for both parties.
  3. Official auction house is automated; chest-to-chest trades must have a mod witness.

7. Accounts & Security

  1. One main account per person. Storage/AFK alts will be banned.
  2. Account sharing or selling = permanent ban.
  3. Suspicious activity (IP hopping, VPN spam) → temporary lock until verification.

8. Building Rules

  1. No hate symbols, real-world politics, or NSFW builds.
  2. Claim size limit: 100 × 100 × 256. Bigger bases require staff approval.
  3. Farm size cap: 3 × 3 chunks. Anything killing TPS will be removed without refund.

9. Events

  1. During an official event, a moderator’s word is law.
  2. Breaking event-specific rules = immediate kick from the event plus penalties (see §10).

11. Appeals

  1. You have 7 days to appeal in Discord #appeals.
  2. Required: IGN / date / reason / explanation / screenshots.
  3. Threats, profanity or lying in an appeal = automatic denial + doubled sentence.

LixPlus Towny

Mode: Towny Survival (towns, nations, economy, wars)

Quick gist: Respect claims, pay your taxes, leave other towns alone unless war is declared.

1. Town & Resident Basics

  1. Town = Mayor + Residents. Every new player starts as a Nomad.
  2. A town needs 6 LC to found and 0.8 LC/day upkeep.
  3. Town blocks = 16 × 16 chunks. You get 10 blocks + (# of residents × 8).
  4. Inactive mayors (14-day offline) → deputy auto-promoted; no deputy → town deleted.

2. Plot Permissions

  1. Default: residents can open doors & chests; outsiders = none.
  2. /plot set perm friend:on etc. only if owner is present in chat.
  3. Do not sell plots with hidden hazards/traps → 3-day ban.
  4. Shops must use trade signs; scam signs (price mismatch) = removal + 24 h jail.

3. Taxes & Upkeep

  1. Town tax: max 2 LC per day. Over this = illegal, auto reset + mayor fined.
  2. Nation tax: max 1 LC per town per day.
  3. If a resident’s balance < tax when collection runs, they are kicked (grace: 24 h).
  4. Upkeep withdrawn 05:00 server time daily. Negative town bank → town falls to ruin.

4. PvP & Griefing

AreaPvPExplosionFire spread
Town claimsoffoffoff
Nation capitalsoffoffoff
Wildernessononon
  1. Griefing inside any claim = instant ban.
  2. Claim-border TNT cannons to break blocks inside a town are prohibited.

5. Nations & Diplomacy

  1. A nation costs 25 LC to create; daily upkeep 2 LC.
  2. Nations can ally (no limit) or declare war (max 2 active wars).
  3. War declaration fee: 10 LC. Cool-down between wars: 48 h.
  4. Sieges last 3 h real-time. A town falls when nation bank ≤ 0 or banner captured.
  5. No spawn-camping enemy towns (stay ≥ 50 blocks from /town spawn).

6. Infrastructure & Redstone

  1. Laggy farms (> 500 entities or > 5 hoppers/plot) will be removed.
  2. Redstone clocks must have on/off switch and auto-shut after 15 min.

7. Economy

  1. Server shop buys/sells common blocks; everything else is player-driven.
  2. Cross-town trading recommended via /warp market.
  3. Real-money trades for items/towns → permanent ban.

8. Towny War Events (Optional PvP Weekends)

  1. Fridays 18:00 server time → “War Time” toggles on for 48 h.
  2. Towns may opt-out by /town toggle pvp off 24 h beforehand.
  3. Destroying utility blocks during War Time allowed; must replace if peace treaty signed.

9. Jails

  1. Mayors may build up to 4 jail cells.
  2. Wrongful jailing → mayor fined 5 LC per minute jailed.
  3. Jail evasion exploits → 3-day ban.
