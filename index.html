1. General Rules

  • Paid services are treated as voluntary donations and are non‑refundable.
  • Rules can change at any time without prior notice.
  • You have 4 days to appeal a punishment from the moment it is issued.
  • The administration is not responsible for item losses caused by bugs, network issues, or power outages.

  • All player actions and chat messages are logged.
  • Staff do not joke — follow their instructions.
  • The inspector chooses the software for checks (Discord, AnyDesk, etc.).
  • Repeat violations stack and extend punishment durations.
